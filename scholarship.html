SDA Scholarships

Crawford Adventist Academy Scholarship

This award has been made available to Crawford Adventist Academy students through the generosity of family and friends of, former student, the late Lloyd Christopher Skeen. This award will be presented to a student pursuing post-secondary studies on a full-time basis who has demonstrated leadership skills, academic merit (70%+), a concern for others, involvement at the Academy and in the community, a desire to succeed in studies and financial challenges. Eligible students must be in the final year of high school. Due: January 31st, 2014 Download Application

Nicholas A. Williams Memorial Scholarship

This award has been made available to members of Pathfinders at Mississauga Seventh-Day Adventist Church to commemorate the memory of an exemplary member, Nicholas Williams. This award will be presented to a student pursuing post-secondary studies on a full-time basis who has demonstrated leadership skills, academic merit (70%+), a concern for others, involvement in the church and community, a desire to succeed in studies and financial challenges. Eligible students must be in the final year of high school or enrolled in any year of study at a recognized post- secondary educational institution.Due: January 31st, 2014 Download Application

Patrina Bailey-Holm Memorial Scholarship

This award has been made available to any female student in Ontario pursuing post-secondary studies. This award will be presented to a student pursuing post-secondary studies on a full-time basis who has demonstrated leadership skills, academic merit (70%+), a concern for others, involvement in the community, a desire to succeed in studies and a desire to be a leader in the corporate community upon graduation. Eligible students must be in the final year of high school or enrolled in any year of study at a recognized post-secondary educational institution. Students must be able to demonstrate that they need this scholarship in order to attend post-secondary studies. Due: January 31st, 2014 Download Application
